About ADAGE


ADAGE is being developed at the Games + Learning + Society Center in partnership with The Learning Games Network with funding from a grant from the National Science Foundation (DRL-1119383).

ADAGE is open source with the following licensing agreement. We welcome contributors and pull requests. Please join our developers group for more information.
